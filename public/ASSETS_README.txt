DROP YOUR ASSETS HERE
=====================

Place the following files in this /public folder:

  hero.mp4         — Your assembled cinematic video from CapCut/DaVinci
                     (H.264, 1920x1080, 30fps, muted)

  hero-mobile.jpg  — A single still frame from Scene 07 (or any scene)
                     used as the mobile fallback image (1200x675px recommended)

  logo.svg         — Your Bakersfield Rental Homes logo in SVG format
                     (optional — the Navbar has a built-in wordmark until ready)

Once hero.mp4 is in place, the Hero component will automatically sync
the video to your scroll position via requestAnimationFrame.
